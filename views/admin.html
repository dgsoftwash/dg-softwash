🧪 D&G Chemical Quick Reference Guide

Professional mixing ratios, application methods, and safety notes for all D&G Soft Wash chemicals.
Always wear appropriate PPE and follow product label instructions. When in doubt, start at lower dilution ratios and adjust as needed.

1. After Wash Conditioner — Bleach Neutralizer

PURPOSE: Post-wash bleach neutralizer and surface conditioner
USE ON:
Roofs, siding, driveways, brick — any surface washed with sodium hypochlorite
MIX RATIOS:
• General neutralizing rinse: 2–4 oz per gallon of water
• Heavy bleach residue/roof: 4–6 oz per gallon of water
APPLICATION:
Apply via downstream injector, pump sprayer, or soft wash system after rinsing primary wash. Rinse area thoroughly with water.
DWELL TIME:
30–60 seconds — do not let dry
SAFETY: Wear gloves and eye protection. Avoid direct skin contact. Store away from heat.
NOTES: Always apply after every roof soft wash to fully neutralize bleach before it reaches landscaping or runoff.

2. Aquashine Hydrophobic Rinse Coating

PURPOSE: Super-concentrated hydrophobic rinse-coating for streak-free finish
USE ON:
Vehicle exteriors, painted surfaces, trim, glass, hard surfaces after washing
MIX RATIOS:
• Standard (lightly soiled): 200:1 — 1 oz per 200 oz water
• Heavy duty/barrel system: 120:1 — 1 oz per 120 oz water
• Maximum concentration: 100:1 — 1 oz per 100 oz water
APPLICATION:
Mix to desired ratio and apply via foam cannon, pump sprayer, or auto-dosing system on cool, clean surface. Rinse off immediately.
DWELL TIME:
Apply and rinse immediately — do NOT let dry on surface
SAFETY: Wear gloves. Avoid prolonged skin or eye contact. Keep away from children.
NOTES: Best results on clean, pre-washed surfaces. For best water-beading, dry with drying towel after rinsing.

3. Elemonator Surfactant

PURPOSE: Bleach-stable surfactant and soft wash soap with lemon scent
USE ON:
House wash, roof cleaning, concrete, siding, brick, vinyl, wood, screens, windows
MIX RATIOS:
• Downstream/pump sprayer add-in: 1 oz per gallon of wash mix
• Batch mix (5-gallon): 5 oz Elemonator + 3 gal SH + 2 gal water
• Batch mix (12-gallon): 12 oz Elemonator + 6 gal SH + 6 gal water
APPLICATION:
Add to sodium hypochlorite batch mix or downstream mix. Apply with soft wash system, downstream injector, or pump sprayer.
DWELL TIME:
3–5 minutes (do not let dry in direct sun)
SAFETY: Use PPE — gloves, eye protection, and protective clothing when working with bleach mixes.
NOTES: Always add surfactant AFTER SH when batch mixing. Compatible with bleach — does not reduce SH strength.

4. F-10 Percarbonate Cleaner

PURPOSE: Oxygen-based wood cleaner and UV damage remover
USE ON:
Decks, fences, wood siding, pergolas, docks — pre- or post-strip restoration
MIX RATIOS:
• Light cleaning/maintenance: 4 oz per gallon of water
• Moderate/weathered wood: 6 oz per gallon of water
• Heavy/UV-damaged/gray wood: 8 oz per gallon of water
APPLICATION:
Dissolve powder in warm water. Apply evenly with pump sprayer, brush, or low-pressure washer to wet wood. Agitate with soft brush.
DWELL TIME:
10–20 minutes — keep surface wet; re-apply if drying
SAFETY: Wear gloves and eye protection. Avoid breathing dust when mixing powder. Flush with water if skin contact occurs.
NOTES: Best used after F-18 stripper to brighten and neutralize wood. Pair with F-8 neutralizer on highly acidic surfaces.

5. F-13 Gutter Grenade Anti-Oxidizer

PURPOSE: Professional-strength gutter and aluminum cleaner
USE ON:
Aluminum gutters, soffits, fascia boards, painted aluminum trim, downspouts
MIX RATIOS:
• Light oxidation/maintenance: 50:1 — 1 oz per 50 oz water
• Standard/moderate streaking: 20:1 — 1 oz per 20 oz water
• Heavy/stubborn tiger stripes: 5:1 — 1 oz per 5 oz water
APPLICATION:
Wet surrounding surfaces and landscaping first. Mix to desired ratio. Apply with brush or pump sprayer. Agitate with soft-bristle brush.
DWELL TIME:
1–3 minutes — do NOT let dry on surface
SAFETY: Use PPE for overhead work — gloves, eye protection, face shield. Highly caustic at full strength. Rinse skin immediately if contact occurs.
NOTES: Works best above 60°F. Always test in small hidden area first. Do not use on anodized aluminum. Pre-wet plants and concrete.

6. F-18 MAX Wood Sealer Stripper & Cleaner

PURPOSE: Heavy-duty wood sealer stripper and concrete cleaner
USE ON:
Decks, fences, wood structures (sealer removal); driveways, concrete, rust-stained masonry
MIX RATIOS:
• Concrete/porous surfaces: 1–3 oz per gallon of water
• Wood — non-film, oil-based sealer: 4–6 oz per gallon of water
• Wood — film-forming/thick sealer: 6–12 oz per gallon of water
APPLICATION:
Wet wood or surface before applying. Apply evenly with pump sprayer or brush. Agitate with stiff brush. Rinse thoroughly with pressure washer.
DWELL TIME:
10–30 minutes depending on sealer type — keep surface wet
SAFETY: Highly alkaline — wear gloves, eye protection, and protective clothing. Neutralize surface with F-8 or F-10 after stripping.
NOTES: Always follow with F-10 Percarbonate Cleaner to brighten and neutralize after stripping. For multi-coat sealers, second application may be needed.

7. Fast Cherry Off-Road Wash

PURPOSE: Cherry-scented heavy-duty vehicle and equipment wash
USE ON:
ATVs, UTVs, dirt bikes, trailers, farm equipment, heavy off-road vehicles
MIX RATIOS:
• Foam cannon: 4 oz in cannon — fill remainder with water
• Downstream injector: 8–10 oz per gallon of water
• Pump sprayer/garden hose: 2–4 oz per gallon of water
APPLICATION:
Mix per method above. Apply generously to entire vehicle, working bottom to top. Agitate heavily soiled areas with brush. Rinse top to bottom.
DWELL TIME:
1–3 minutes — keep applying to prevent drying
SAFETY: Wear gloves. Avoid direct eye contact. Safe for most painted surfaces at recommended dilutions.
NOTES: A single 32 oz bottle makes up to 16 gallons of RTU solution. For extremely caked mud, let soak 2–3 minutes before agitating.

8. Mothers Professional Water-Based Degreaser

PURPOSE: Water-based concentrated degreaser — safe, non-toxic
USE ON:
Engine bays, undercarriage, equipment, shop floors, vehicle exteriors, aluminum parts
MIX RATIOS:
• Light to moderate degreasing: 1:4 — 1 part concentrate : 4 parts water
• General purpose: 1:2 — 1 part concentrate : 2 parts water
• Heavy/engine degreasing: 1:1 — full strength (equal parts)
APPLICATION:
Mix per desired dilution. Apply to cool surface with pump sprayer or brush. Agitate with brush on heavy grime. Rinse with pressure washer.
DWELL TIME:
2–5 minutes — longer for heavy build-up
SAFETY: Non-toxic and biodegradable. Wear gloves for extended use. Will not stain paint or harm aluminum.
NOTES: For engine bays, cover sensitive electrical components before application. Rinse thoroughly to prevent residue.

9. Preserver Pro Hydrophobic UV Protector

PURPOSE: Super-concentrated professional UV protector and hydrophobic coating
USE ON:
Gutters, siding, painted surfaces, windows, window frames, vinyl, aluminum trim, exterior surfaces
MIX RATIOS:
• Standard application: See product label — super concentrated
• Typical range: 50:1–100:1 dilution ratios
• Touchup/light coat: Higher dilution per label instructions
APPLICATION:
Mix per label instructions. Apply to clean, dry surface with pump sprayer, soft cloth, or foam applicator. Spread evenly and allow to bond.
DWELL TIME:
Per label — typically 1–3 minutes; do not let over-dwell in direct sun
SAFETY: Wear gloves and eye protection. Avoid prolonged skin contact. Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
NOTES: For best UV protection, apply after washing and before extended direct sunlight exposure. Re-apply seasonally for maximum protection.
