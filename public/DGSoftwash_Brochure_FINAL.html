❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is soft washing safe for my plants?

Absolutely! We pre-wet all vegetation and use SH Neutralizer to protect your landscaping. Our biodegradable solutions are designed to be plant-safe when properly applied.

How long will the results last?

Soft wash results typically last 4-6 times longer than pressure washing because we kill organic growth at the root level, not just blast it off the surface.

What's included in hydrophobic treatments?

Our hydrophobic UV protectant creates a water-repelling barrier that helps surfaces stay cleaner longer. Window treatments provide streak-free, water-beading protection for months.

Do you offer commercial services?

Yes! We service office buildings, restaurants, retail centers, apartment complexes, and HOA communities. Custom quotes provided for all commercial projects.

Are you licensed and insured?

Fully licensed, insured, and bonded in Virginia. We carry comprehensive liability insurance for your protection and peace of mind.

What areas do you serve?
